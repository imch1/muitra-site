Our mission

A home that feels fair.

Muitra exists to take the mental load off one person's shoulders — and share it, quietly and fairly, between everyone who lives there.

⚖️

Sharing, not scorekeeping

Most homes run on one person remembering everything. Muitra turns that invisible work into a shared rhythm — no nagging, and no tally to keep in your head.

🧠

The mental load is real work

Deciding what needs doing, and when, is a job in itself. We make that job visible and split it — not just the chores, but the remembering.

🤝

Built for partners and households

Whether you're two or six, Muitra keeps turns balanced automatically — so your home runs on trust instead of resentment.

🌱

Small habits, calmer home

A fairer home is built from tiny, repeatable moments. Muitra protects those habits so daily life feels lighter for everyone.
