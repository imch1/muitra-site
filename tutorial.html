How Muitra works

A shared home runs on small habits. Here's the whole idea in seven short steps.

1 🏠

A shared home, one list.

In Muitra, tasks take turns between everyone at home. Here's the quick tour.

2 ✅

Whose turn?

Today shows what's yours. Tap to complete — and the turn passes to whoever's next.

3 🤝

Got their back.

Help finishes someone else's task for them. Cover hands one over for later.

4 ➕

Build your rhythm.

Tap + to add a task and how often it repeats. Start a custom task's name with an emoji to spot it at a glance in the Calendar.

5 🗓️

Two ways to look ahead.

Tap Week for a day-by-day agenda, or Month for the whole picture at a glance — each task colored by who's on it.

6 ⚖️

Always fair.

Muitra simply keeps turns balanced — just the two of you, or a whole household, each with their own color.

7 🎉

You're all set.

That's the tour. Open Today and let your home run itself.
